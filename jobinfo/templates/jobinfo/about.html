About

The Job Information System was created and designed to help job seekers and job recruiters keep better track of job applications. The job hunting process can be very tedious and there are very few online tools to help people keep track of places they have applied/ received applications from. As part, this application allows both recruiters and job seekers a more clean way to keep track of what companies they have applied to/ received applications from rather than outdated methods like a spreadsheet or hand written notes! This application is implemented using Django.
